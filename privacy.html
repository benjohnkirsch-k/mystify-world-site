Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Mystify World AI Publisher collects, uses, protects, stores and manages information when users access our website or use our AI-powered publishing platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate Mystify World AI Publisher, deliver requested services, maintain platform security, and improve user experience.

  • Basic account information provided during sign-in.
  • Public profile information made available through authorized platforms.
  • OAuth authorization tokens required to perform approved publishing actions.
  • Content created, uploaded, or generated through the platform.
  • Technical information such as browser type, operating system, device information, and IP address.
  • Usage logs required for security, troubleshooting, and service improvement.
We only request information that is necessary for providing the features selected by the user.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information collected through Mystify World AI Publisher is used exclusively to operate the platform and provide requested services.

  • Generate AI-assisted content.
  • Create stories, scripts, captions, and publishing drafts.
  • Publish content only after explicit user authorization.
  • Maintain account security and detect unauthorized access.
  • Improve platform performance and reliability.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

3. TikTok Authorization

Mystify World AI Publisher uses the official TikTok OAuth authorization system whenever a user chooses to connect a TikTok account.

Users explicitly authorize access before any permissions are granted. The application never requests unnecessary permissions.

  • No TikTok password is collected.
  • Authorization may be revoked by the user at any time.
  • Publishing actions are performed only after user approval.
  • Only permissions approved by TikTok are requested.

4. Other Third-Party Platforms

Mystify World AI Publisher may integrate with official APIs provided by third-party platforms such as YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, or other supported services.

Your use of these platforms remains subject to their own Privacy Policies and Terms of Service.

  • YouTube APIs
  • Facebook APIs
  • Instagram APIs
  • Future officially supported platforms

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve security, remember user preferences, maintain authenticated sessions, and enhance overall website performance.

  • Authentication cookies
  • Security cookies
  • Performance cookies
  • User preference cookies

Users may disable cookies through their browser settings, although certain features of the platform may become unavailable.

6. Data Security

Protecting user information is a fundamental priority. We implement appropriate administrative, technical, and organizational security measures designed to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

  • Encrypted HTTPS connections.
  • Official OAuth authentication.
  • Access control mechanisms.
  • Routine security monitoring.
  • Secure infrastructure practices.
Although no internet service can guarantee absolute security, we continuously work to maintain a high level of protection for user data.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

  • Account information is retained while the account remains active.
  • OAuth tokens are stored only as required for authorized services.
  • Users may request deletion of their information at any time.

8. Data Sharing

Mystify World AI Publisher does not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

Information may only be shared when required to operate official third-party services requested by the user or when required by applicable law.

  • No sale of personal information.
  • No advertising based on personal data.
  • No unauthorized disclosure of user information.

9. Your Rights

Depending on applicable laws, users may have the right to:

  • Access their personal information.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of personal information.
  • Withdraw previously granted authorization.
  • Request a copy of stored information where applicable.

10. Data Deletion Requests

Users may request deletion of their account and associated information by contacting us using the email address below.

Once the request has been verified, information will be removed within a reasonable period unless retention is required by law.

11. Children's Privacy

Mystify World AI Publisher is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that such information has been collected, appropriate steps will be taken to remove it.

12. International Data Transfers

Depending on your location and the services used, information may be processed or stored in different countries where our service providers operate. Reasonable safeguards are applied to protect user information during such transfers.

13. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect improvements, changes in legal requirements, or modifications to our services. The latest version will always be published on this page.
